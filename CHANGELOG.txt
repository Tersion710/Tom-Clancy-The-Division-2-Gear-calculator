--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  NEW FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Weapon & Gear Talent Locking:
  - Exotic weapons now show only their unique locked talent in the dropdown
    (42 exotic weapons updated — all exotics now have correct talent locked)
  - Named weapons with a "Perfect" themed talent now show only that talent
    (73 named weapons updated)
  - Named weapons without a locked talent continue to show the full list
  - Selecting an exotic or named weapon auto-selects its correct talent
  - Fix applies to both Desktop and Cozy theme weapon views

  Attachment Filtering by Weapon Type:
  - Each weapon now only shows compatible attachments in its dropdowns
  - ARs see 5.56 muzzles and 5.56 magazines
  - SMGs see 9mm or .45 ACP muzzles and magazines based on caliber
  - LMGs see belt-fed magazines only
  - MMRs see marksman-specific magazines and high-magnification scopes
  - Pistols see pistol-specific underbarrels, magazines, and muzzles
  - Shotguns see tubular spring magazines
  - Scopes and underbarrels are filtered to compatible weapon types
  - Exotic weapons with fixed mods show those slots as read-only

  Gear Attribute Filtering by Slot:
  - Each gear slot now only shows attributes that can actually roll on it
  - Gloves: Offensive attributes only (Crit Chance, Crit Damage, Headshot
    Damage, Weapon Handling, Protection from Elites, Damage to Health)
  - Kneepads: Defensive attributes + limited Utility (Skill Haste,
    Skill Duration, Status Effects) — no offensive attributes
  - Holster: Offensive + limited Defensive (Armor on Kill, Incoming Repairs,
    Hazard Protection, Health, Pulse Resistance) + limited Utility
    (Skill Haste, Status Effects)
  - Mask, Chest, Backpack: Full attribute pool available
